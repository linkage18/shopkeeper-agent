# 客户最多的雇员

## 定义
统计每个雇员（销售支持代表）所服务的客户数量，并找出客户数量最多的雇员的全名。

## 涉及表
- customers
- employees

## 示例SQL
```sql
SELECT CONCAT(e.first_name, ' ', e.last_name) AS full_name FROM customers c JOIN employees e ON c.support_rep_id = e.id GROUP BY e.id, e.first_name, e.last_name ORDER BY COUNT(*) DESC LIMIT 1
```
