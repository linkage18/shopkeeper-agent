# 拥有最多客户的雇员定义

## 定义
通过 customers 表中的 support_rep_id 字段关联 employees 表，统计每个雇员作为客户支持代表所负责的客户数量，取客户数最多的雇员全名。

## 涉及表
- employees
- customers

## 示例SQL
```sql
SELECT CONCAT(e.first_name, ' ', e.last_name) AS 全名 FROM employees e JOIN customers c ON e.id = c.support_rep_id GROUP BY e.id, e.first_name, e.last_name HAVING COUNT(*) = (SELECT MAX(cnt) FROM (SELECT COUNT(*) AS cnt FROM customers GROUP BY support_rep_id) t)
```
